Pesto Steak

Description

Easy, four ingredient pesto flank steak recipe!

Ingredients

  • 3/2 lb flank steak
  • 1/2 cup pesto
  • 2 Tbsp white vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp water

Steps

  1. Mix together the pesto, vinegar and water.
  2. Sprinkle both sides of meat with coarse salt and allow steak to sit at room temperature for 30-40 minutes before grilling
  3. Grill to desired temperature. We like our meat medium which is 140-145 Degrees F. Depending on the thickness of your steak it will be about 5 minutes per side.
  4. A few minutes before it is done brush both sides with pesto.
  5. Remove from grill and allow to sit at room temperature for 5 minutes or so.
  6. Slice steak against the grain and drizzle with remaining pesto mixture.
